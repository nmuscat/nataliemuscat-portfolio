Background & Story

About

Most weeks you'll find me somewhere in the American West or Baja — working remotely as a Data Engineer from a desert camp or a trailhead, with my dog Leo riding co-pilot.
After my built Wrangler was stolen in 2025, I started fresh with a Jeep Gladiator Rubicon and haven't looked back. I'm a Trail Guide for onX Offroad, a Tread Lightly! Trainer, a Rebelle Rally 2023 competitor, and part of the KC HiLiTES and Rugged Radios teams.
I believe in pushing limits, traveling responsibly, and building real community through shared adventure.
